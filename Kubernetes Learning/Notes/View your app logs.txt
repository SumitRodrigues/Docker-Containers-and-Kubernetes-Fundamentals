Listing Pods: Use the command kubectl get pods -n development to list all the pods running your application.
Viewing Logs: To inspect the logs of a specific pod, use kubectl logs [pod-name] -n development.
Debugging: Application logs are useful for debugging issues and monitoring HTTP requests, helping ensure your application is functioning correctly.

This video demonstrates how to access and use application logs to troubleshoot and verify the health of your Kubernetes pods.
